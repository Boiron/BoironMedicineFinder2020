Warnings & Information

Blisters are caused by friction or burns. Immediately place the burned area under cold water to limit the extent of the burn. Do not pierce a blister unless it prevents you from using your hands or walking. See a doctor if pain or redness increases, or if pus appears.

Calendula Burn

Temporarily protects and helps promote healing of minor burns.

Find Near You
Dosage:

For minor burns, immediately run cool water on the burn for several minutes and pat dry. . Then apply a thin layer of Calendula Burn to affected area 3 times a day or as needed. For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do not use if you are allergic to Calendula officinalis or to any of this product's inactive ingredients. Do not use under tight bandages. Ask a doctor before use in case of deep or puncture wounds, animal bites or serious burns that present blisters, thickening of the skin, white and leathery apprearance. Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists for more than 3 days or worsens or reoccurs. Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.
