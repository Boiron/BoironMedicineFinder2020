Warnings & Information

Spider bites usually cause a red, indurated rash around the two small bite marks. Try to catch the spider for identification. Disinfect the area properly, and protect with a bandage if necessary. Immediately see a doctor if pain is severe, if infection occurs, or if you experience abdominal cramping or excessive sweating around the mark or on the entire limb. See a doctor if healing is slow or if the wound worsens.
