Temporarily protects and helps promote healing of minor burns.

Dosage:

For minor burns, immediately run cool water on the burn for several minutes and pat dry. . Then apply a thin layer of Calendula Burn to affected area 3 times a day or as needed. For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Do not use if you are allergic to Calendula officinalis or to any of this product's inactive ingredients. Do not use under tight bandages.
