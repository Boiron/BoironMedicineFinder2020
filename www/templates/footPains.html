Arnicare FootCare

Temporarily relieves sore feet and other aches and pains due to overexertion and minor injuries. Reduces pain, swelling and discoloration from bruises associated with spains and strains.

Dosage:

Apply a thin layer of Arnicare FootCare to affected area and massage gently 3 times a day or as needed. If heat or ice is applied, wait 5 minutes before applying Arnicare FootCare. For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes, mucous membranes, damaged or irritated skin or wounds. Do not use if you are allergic to Arnica montana or to any of this product's inactive ingredients. When using this product, use only as directed; do not tightly wrap or bandage the treated area, do not apply heat or ice to the treated area immediately before or after use. Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists for more than 3 days or worsens. Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.
