Warnings & Information

Hay fever is caused by your particular sensitivity to allergens (pollens, molds, dust mites, cat's saliva, etc.). Try to eliminate the source of allergens to decrease the frequency and intensity of hay fever. If facial pain or tenderness occurs, or if the discharge changes from watery to thick and purulent, see a doctor.
